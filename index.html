Dear Robin,

Happy 66th. After miscalculating time, as per usual, I had to pull an all-nighter on this present.

You have a rare feel for time and rhythm. You also know how easy it is to get lost in the work. In the details. In the moment. Over time, your balance and rhythm might rub off on me.

Thank you for welcoming Diko and me with your open heart and kind soul. It means more than I can say. Du sätter guldkant på tillvaron. Your presence makes life feel more precious, just by being there.

Furthermore, you have changed my life, and I hope to one day be half the man you are.

We love you very much and look forward to what is ahead. The four of us. Together.

Eternally yours, with all our love,
Dikoros & Dorian!
